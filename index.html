ASTRAFIX

AstraFix is ​​proposed as a web and mobile application that will seek to optimize the time of attention between an expert and a client. Since, clients will download said application and immediately associate the means of payment for a token called Fixtoken.

We will introduce a new payment method for our service platform with FIXToken

One of the problems that currently arise in the service sector is the lack of finding the right person to solve a problem in your home, either due to the lack of trained personnel or organization, as well as the little option they have some service providers such as: (plumbers, carpenters, construction foremen and electricians etc) to receive payments through means other than cash.

Against this background, there are no providers that have developed techniques that allow them to improve their customer service, thinking about those days and special dates in which there is greater congestion in this type of business and it becomes more complex to provide an appropriate service to each one of the clients. This shows an absence of strategies to improve these situations and, therefore, processes such as timely and adequate attention by staff, different options when making a payment are difficult.
